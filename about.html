Company overview

A Multi-Sector Chemical Solutions Partner

ChemaTex is a Cairo-based chemical manufacturer and supplier with over 25 years of experience in industrial chemical solutions. The company was established with a strong focus on textile auxiliaries and has since expanded into industrial detergents, personal care raw materials, sanitizers, disinfectants, reverse osmosis water treatment chemicals, and general raw materials.

Our strength lies in combining technical knowledge with reliable supply. Through formulation experience, quality control, and a practical understanding of client needs, ChemaTex supports manufacturers across different sectors with products designed for consistency, performance, and value.
